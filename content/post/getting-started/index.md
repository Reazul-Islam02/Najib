This workshop is designed for early-career reporters and students who want to build strong investigative habits.

## What participants learn

- How to define a newsroom-worthy investigation.
- How to track documents, sources, and timelines.
- How to verify claims using open records and public data.
- How to structure long-form stories with clear evidence.

## Format

- Duration: 2 days
- Mode: In-person
- Level: Beginner to intermediate

Participants leave with a complete pitch outline and a reporting checklist they can use in future investigations.
