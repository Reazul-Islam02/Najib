This session introduces practical data journalism methods for local reporting teams.

## Core topics

- Finding and evaluating open datasets.
- Cleaning tabular data for newsroom use.
- Building simple thematic maps.
- Avoiding misleading visuals and charts.

## Deliverable

Participants create one map-driven story draft with a data note that explains methods and limitations.
