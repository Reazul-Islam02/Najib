Great photojournalism is more than a strong image. It requires context, ethics, and a clear editorial angle.

## Training focus

- Planning visual coverage before assignment.
- Composing images that support narrative flow.
- Writing accurate captions and metadata.
- Editing a coherent photo essay from field material.

## Final exercise

Participants submit a five-image story sequence with captions and a short editorial rationale.
