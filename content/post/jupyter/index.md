Mobile reporting is now a core newsroom skill. This workshop helps participants produce professional short-form news packages using only a phone.

## Workshop modules

- Shot planning for interviews and field scenes.
- Clean audio capture in noisy environments.
- Fast editing workflows for same-day publishing.
- Caption writing and platform-first distribution.

## Outcome

Each participant completes one publishable 60-90 second news story by the end of the session.
